Questions

Frequently asked

Does this work for newborns, or is it for older babies?

+

It works for both! The guide covers newborn through 6 months. Each stage has its own cry patterns and body language cues, and the guide walks you through each one clearly.

Is this based on the Dunstan Baby Language method?

+

No. This guide focuses entirely on observable behaviors — body language, facial cues, timing, and context. No need to train your ear to specific sounds.

How quickly can I download and read the guide?

What if it doesn't work for my baby?

+

Every baby is different. What this guide does is give you a framework to observe and respond — which significantly reduces the guesswork. Most parents notice a real difference within the first few days.
